Biography

I am a postdoc at Cornell University but will soon become a postdoc at the University of South Carolina at its new disease institute. I received my PhD at Penn State University working with Ottar Bjornstad. I am fascinated by everything ecological (disease, insects, everything). My hobbies include theater (opera and musicals), video games (I’m a huge Bioware fangirl but love my Baldur’s Gate 3), and getting swole at the gym!
